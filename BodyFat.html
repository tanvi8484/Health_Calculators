Understanding Body Fat

Body fat percentage is a measure of the amount of fat in your body relative to your total body weight. It is an important indicator of health and fitness.

Generally, a body fat percentage of:

  • 10-20% is considered healthy for men.
  • 20-30% is considered healthy for women.

Consult with a healthcare provider for personalized advice.
