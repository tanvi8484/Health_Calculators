Waist to Height Ratio Calculator

The waist-to-height ratio (WHtR) is a simple measure of health that can help assess your risk of developing health problems related to obesity. It is calculated by dividing your waist circumference by your height.

Understanding Your Result

A WHtR of:

  • Less than 0.4: Low risk of health problems
  • 0.4 to 0.5: Moderate risk
  • Greater than 0.5: High risk
