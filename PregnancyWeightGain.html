Weight Gain Recommendations:

Weight gain during pregnancy is important for the health of both mother and baby. The recommended weight gain varies based on your pre-pregnancy BMI:

  • Underweight (BMI < 18.5): 12.5 to 18 kg
  • Normal weight (BMI 18.5 - 24.9): 11.5 to 16 kg
