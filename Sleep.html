    About Sleep

    Getting enough sleep is crucial for your physical and mental health. Adults typically need between 7-9 hours of sleep each night. Sleep cycles last about 90 minutes, and waking up at the end of a cycle can help you feel more refreshed.

    By using this calculator, you can find the best times to go to bed to ensure you're completing full sleep cycles before waking up.
