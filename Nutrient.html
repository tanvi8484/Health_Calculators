About Nutrients

Nutrients are substances that provide nourishment essential for the maintenance of life and for growth. The main types of nutrients include:

  • Carbohydrates: The body's main source of energy.
  • Proteins: Essential for building and repairing tissues.
  • Fats: Important for hormone production and nutrient absorption.
  • Vitamins and Minerals: Crucial for various body functions and processes.
